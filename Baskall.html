History of Basketball

Basketball was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts. It has grown into one of the most popular sports in the world.

Basic Rules of Basketball

  • The game is played with two teams of five players each.
  • The objective is to score points by shooting the basketball through the opponent's hoop.
  • A field goal is worth two points, or three points if shot from beyond the three-point line.
  • Each team has a 24-second shot clock to attempt a shot.

Player Positions

There are five primary positions in basketball:

  • Point Guard: Often the team's primary ball-handler. ( ME PLAY THIS)
  • Shooting Guard: Known for scoring and shooting skills.
  • Small Forward: Versatile player, often a mix of skills.
  • Power Forward: Plays near the basket, combining strength and agility.
  • Center: The tallest player, usually plays in the key area.
